FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about ITB POS and how it can help your restaurant.

What hardware do I need for ITB POS?

ITB POS is completely hardware agnostic. You can use any tablet (iPad or Android), smartphone, laptop, or desktop computer. We also support standard receipt printers, cash drawers, and kitchen display systems. No proprietary hardware is required - use what you already have or choose from our recommended options.

Can I use ITB POS offline?

Yes! ITB POS has a robust offline mode. When your internet goes down, the system continues to work seamlessly. All orders, payments, and data are stored locally and automatically sync to the cloud once connectivity is restored. You'll never miss a sale due to internet issues.

How does inventory tracking work?

Our inventory system tracks ingredients at the recipe level. When you sell a dish, the system automatically deducts the ingredients used. You can set low-stock alerts, track waste, and generate purchase orders automatically. The system also calculates your actual food costs vs. theoretical costs to identify discrepancies.

What payment methods are supported?

ITB POS supports all major payment methods including credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), mobile payments (Apple Pay, Google Pay), and local payment options. In Bangladesh, we integrate with bKash, Nagad, and Rocket. For international payments, we support Stripe, Razorpay, and other gateways.

Is training and support included?

Absolutely! All plans include free onboarding, training sessions for your staff, and 24/7 customer support. We provide video tutorials, documentation, and a dedicated success manager for enterprise clients. Our average support response time is under 2 minutes.

Can I manage multiple restaurant locations?

Yes! ITB POS is designed for multi-location management. You get a centralized dashboard to monitor all restaurants in real-time, manage menus across locations (with the ability to customize per location), consolidated reporting, and centralized staff management. Scale from 1 to 1000+ locations with ease.
