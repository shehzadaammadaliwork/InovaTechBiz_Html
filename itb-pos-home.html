FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ITB POS.

What is ITB POS?

ITB POS is an all-in-one restaurant management system that includes point-of-sale, online ordering, delivery management, inventory tracking, staff management, and CRM features. It's designed to help restaurants of all sizes streamline operations, reduce costs, and increase revenue. Whether you run a single food truck or a chain of restaurants, ITB POS scales with your business.

How much does ITB POS cost?

ITB POS offers flexible pricing plans starting with a free trial. Our Starter plan begins at $29/month for single locations. Professional plans with advanced features like inventory management and delivery integration are $79/month. Enterprise plans with multi-location management and custom integrations are available at custom pricing. All plans include 24/7 support and free updates.

Does ITB POS work offline?

Yes! ITB POS has a robust offline mode. You can continue taking orders, processing cash payments, and managing your restaurant even without internet connection. All your data is stored locally and automatically syncs to the cloud when your connection returns. This ensures you never miss a sale due to internet outages.

What payment methods does ITB POS support?

ITB POS supports all major payment methods including credit and debit cards through integrations with Stripe, Square, and other processors. In Bangladesh, we offer native integration with bKash, Nagad, and Rocket. We also support cash payments, split bills, gift cards, and loyalty points redemption. Contact us for custom payment integration needs.

Can I integrate ITB POS with delivery apps?

Absolutely! ITB POS integrates with major delivery platforms including DoorDash, Uber Eats, Grubhub, and regional services like Foodpanda and Pathao in South Asia. Orders from these platforms flow directly into your kitchen without manual entry. You can also manage your own delivery fleet with our built-in driver management and real-time tracking features.

How long does setup take?

Most restaurants are up and running within 24-48 hours. Our onboarding team helps you import your menu, set up payment processing, train your staff, and configure all features. For larger chains, we offer dedicated implementation support. We also provide pre-built menu templates for quick setup and free data migration from your existing POS system.
