FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about ITB POS in Bangladesh.

Does ITB POS support bKash payments?

Yes! ITB POS has native bKash merchant integration. You can accept payments via QR code scan or phone number entry. Transactions are confirmed instantly, and settlement to your bank account is automatic. We're an official bKash merchant partner.

Is there Bengali language support?

Yes, ITB POS offers full Bengali (বাংলা) language support. The entire interface, menus, receipts, and reports can all be displayed in Bengali. Your staff can work in the language they're most comfortable with, reducing training time and errors.

How does VAT compliance work?

ITB POS automatically calculates VAT according to NBR (National Board of Revenue) guidelines. Invoices are generated in the required format for compliance. At tax time, you can export VAT reports that are ready for submission, making tax filing simple and error-free.

Can I integrate with Foodpanda and Pathao?

Yes! ITB POS integrates with Bangladesh's top food delivery platforms including Foodpanda and Pathao Food. Orders from these platforms flow directly into your kitchen display system without manual entry, reducing errors and speeding up fulfillment.

What if my internet connection is unreliable?

ITB POS has a robust offline mode designed for areas with unreliable internet connectivity. When your connection goes down, the system continues working normally. All orders, payments, and data are stored locally and automatically sync to the cloud once connectivity is restored.

Do you have local support in Dhaka?

Yes! We have a dedicated support team based in Gulshan, Dhaka. Get help in Bengali or English via phone (+880 1712-345-678), live chat, or WhatsApp. For Professional and Enterprise plans, we also offer in-person training and setup at your restaurant location.
